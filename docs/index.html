From Prussia with Love

The Journey of Beate Zülsdorf

About This Project

This interactive map traces the remarkable life journey of Beate Zülsdorf (later Beate Owens), from her birth in West Prussia in 1941, through the harrowing evacuation during World War II, her childhood in Soviet-occupied East Germany, escape to West Germany, and eventual immigration to America in 1963 where she became a Pan American World Airways stewardess. Click on any location marker or timeline entry to read that chapter of her story.
